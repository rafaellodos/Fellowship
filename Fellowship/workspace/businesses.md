## AVENOIRTECH

**What it is:** AI operations company. Builds and deploys AI systems for home service contractors — automating intake, lead qualification, scheduling, and client communication for businesses that have no technical resources to do this themselves.

**Current stage:** ACTIVE BUILD — MVP pilot with Plenty Pavers

**Key facts:**
- Primary market: Brazilian contractors in Orlando, Florida
- Product: Voice agent system (multilingual: English, Brazilian Portuguese, Spanish)
- Tech stack: Vapi.ai (phone agent) + Make.com (automation) + Claude API (lead scoring) + Airtable (operations data) + Twilio (SMS alerts)
- Business model: Setup fee + monthly retainer
- Current pilot: Plenty Pavers (family paving business in Orlando)
- Week 1 scope: Core loop only (Vapi → Make → Claude → Airtable → Twilio)
- Deferred to Week 2: Twilio sequences, Calendly, dashboards, nurture flows

**Positioning:** AI operations partner for contractors who can't build this themselves. Not a general AI consultancy — specific to home service contractor automation.
